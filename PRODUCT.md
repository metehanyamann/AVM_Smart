# AVM Smart Track

## Product Purpose
Real-time multi-camera face recognition, floor traffic analytics, and criminal alert system for shopping mall security operations.

## Users
1. **Security operators** — monitor 3+ camera feeds simultaneously, respond to wanted-person alerts, track confirmed identities
2. **Mall management** — review floor traffic analytics, peak hour reports, cross-floor movement patterns
3. **Academic reviewers** — professors and jury members evaluating the system as a graduation project

## Register
product

## Brand
- Name: AVM Smart Track
- Tone: Industrial authority — precise, data-dense, purposeful. Not flashy. The screen needs to convey competence.
- Anti-references: generic blue/purple SaaS dashboard, glassmorphism, emoji icon soup, Inter font on dark background

## Strategic Principles
- Data at a glance: every visible metric must be immediately readable from 3 meters
- No decoration for decoration's sake: every visual element serves the operator
- Presentation-ready: suitable for displaying to executives and academics without apology
